# 📊 Customer Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Customer Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Tableau Public project designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and business trends. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful visual insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

This project demonstrates how data visualization can be used to uncover patterns in customer activity, sales distribution, payment preferences, and market performance through an intuitive and interactive dashboard.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance and revenue trends.
* Identify top-performing shopping malls and sales locations.
* Understand customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
* Evaluate product category performance.
* Examine payment method preferences.
* Track monthly sales trends and business growth patterns.
* Support data-driven business decision-making through visual analytics.
